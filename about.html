The Story of Aurelia

Crafting spectacular transitions for high-net-worth individuals and families with exceptional tastes.

Founded in 1998, Aurelia began with a simple but radical premise: luxury real estate shouldn't just be about property transactions. It should be an elegant partnership designed to protect client confidentiality while offering exclusive, curated entry into off-market sanctuaries.

Over the last quarter-century, our small boutique firm has grown into an international powerhouse with representation in key global capitals—from Manhattan and Beverly Hills to Monaco, Geneva, and Tokyo. Today, we handle billions in transactional assets annually while maintaining the signature boutique discretion that our patrons demand.

Our Sovereign Mission

Our mission is to simplify the acquisition and stewardship of ultra-luxury estates. We serve as strict fiduciaries to our clientele, ensuring that every asset we present is thoroughly vetted, legally sound, and perfectly aligned with the preservation and elevation of their private lifestyle.

Our Sovereign Vision

To remain the world's most trusted gatekeeper to off-market, premier properties. We aim to consistently pioneer advanced financial structures and architectural analytics, enabling high-society families to pass down legendary legacies through generation-defining global estates.
