Frequently Queried Protocols

Understanding how Aurelia handles private acquisitions guarantees absolute safety and alignment with your family office standards.

Do you handle off-market property acquisitions under strict NDAs?

Absolutely. Over 60% of our ultra-luxury transactions (exceeding $15M value) never surface publicly. We routinely execute multi-party Non-Disclosure Agreements, negotiate via designated family trusts, and utilize masked offshore entities to keep purchaser identity entirely anonymous.

How do we schedule a private architectural walking tour?

Private viewings can be facilitated globally. Following a standard financial verification or pre-approval certificate verification process, we organize chartered flights, helipad land arrangements, and complete security credentials for our buyers.

What is the typical financial verification process?

To protect the security and integrity of our structural estates, prospective buyers are requested to provide a confidential Letter of Credit or proof of capacity from a recognized tier-one international bank prior to sharing exact geographic coordinate maps.
