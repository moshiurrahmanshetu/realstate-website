Project Overview

A magnificent cliffside marvel that effortlessly binds architectural ingenuity with the organic topology of the French Riviera.

Villa de l'Horizon is a statement of peerless luxury, suspended 150 meters above the shimmering azure waters of Nice. Designed by award-winning architect Jean-Louis Maçon, the property integrates high-performance bioclimatic structural shells with generous custom glass curtain walls, delivering an uninterrupted 270-degree panorama of the coastal horizons.

From the handcrafted French white oak floorboards to the customized dual-elevation heated infinity pool with direct underwater soundscapes, every square foot of this estate represents absolute mastercraft. Perfect for high-level entertaining, it incorporates full staff service wings with individual service routes to ensure ultimate discretion for family members and honored VIP guests.
