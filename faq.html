Acquisitions & Buying

What is the initial process for purchasing a property with Aurelia?

The process begins with an exclusive consultation with one of our senior advisors. To align with security and respect, prospective purchasers must complete a basic background verification or present a confidential bank reference before detailed architectural plans, coordinate files, or off-market registries are shared.

Can acquisitions be executed via legal trusts or offshore entities?

Absolutely. We specialize in coordinating high-value acquisitions with family offices, legal councils, and estate trustees. Our in-house legal experts routinely structure acquisitions utilizing designated trusts, LLCs, and international holdings to protect buyer privacy.

Are your property valuations certified by third parties?

Yes. Every listing in our curated collections features rigorous independent valuation reports provided by leading international firms. We also offer comprehensive bioclimatic assessments and structural integrity certifications for elite properties.

Do you coordinate cross-border transactions?

Our brokerage operates seamlessly across multiple jurisdictions including Switzerland, France, Monaco, and the United States. We manage international wire clearings, sovereign tax declarations, and legal residency routing associated with elite property acquisitions.

Discretion & Security

How do you protect buyer and seller anonymity?

Anonymity is our core foundation. Over 60% of our portfolio consists of off-market "pocket listings" that are never published publicly. We enforce strict, legally binding non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) on all parties, filter property tours, and utilize secure, private channels for all client communications.

Are secure private viewings available for sovereign individuals?

Yes. We facilitate fully private, secure property viewings for qualified acquisition parties. This includes arranging discreet transport (such as helicopter arrivals directly to onsite helipads), dedicated security details, and customized walkthroughs led by certified structural architects.

How is user-entered digital information managed?

Our online servers utilize corporate-grade transport encryption (SSL/TLS). Digital inquiries and client profile records are stored on isolated, encrypted cloud databases and are strictly restricted to assigned senior acquisition officers. No client data is ever sold or shared with external marketing networks.

Architectural & Design Standards

What defines a "bioclimatic" property in your catalog?

Bioclimatic properties are masterpieces engineered to interact harmoniously with natural local elements. They feature passive solar heating, natural ventilation corridors, high-performance thermal insulation (such as double-shell concrete facades), and optimized thermal mass blocks to maintain comfort with near-zero energy consumption.

Are custom interior design modifications supported?

Yes. For properties still undergoing final curation or under development, we connect purchasers with elite international interior design houses. Buyers can customize primary stone choices (such as book-matched Carrara marbles), timber finishes, security vault placements, and private wellness center configurations.

How do you verify the smart home infrastructure?

Our properties feature state-of-the-art integrated automation servers. We perform specialized audits on central heating, ventilating, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems, biometric entry locks, professional-grade home theaters, and automated perimeter thermal cameras to guarantee flawless operation upon handover.

Elite Concierge Services

What is the "Elite Members Desk" availability?

The Elite Members Desk provides unrestricted 24/7/365 concierge support for qualified owners of our premium estates. This private desk handles immediate property maintenance routing, secure transport arrangements, localized private dining staffing, and administrative needs.

Do you coordinate charter services for international clients?

Yes. We maintain close alliances with leading global private aviation networks, luxury yacht operators, and professional security firms. We can coordinate door-to-door transit schedules from your home port directly to the target estate.

How are off-market listings sourced for Elite buyers?

Elite buyers receive a personalized acquisition strategist who proactively communicates with private landowners, châteaux owners, and estate managers across desired coordinates to negotiate custom pocket acquisitions tailored specifically to your design criteria.
